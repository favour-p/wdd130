Family Float (class II-III)

This is a calm and relaxing rafting experience perfect for families, beginners, and guest who want to enjoy beautiful river scenery without fast rapids. It's a great way to introduce children or first-time rafters to the water while still having fun and learning basic padding skills.

  • Gentle currents ideal for all ages
  • Professional guide steering the raft
  • Great opportunities for photos and sightseeing
  • Includes safety gears
